Enviroplus promotional design

Another brand belonging to Abco is Enviroplus, with its extensive range of chemical-free natural cleaning solutions that deliver powerful results for environmentally-conscious cleaners and facilities across Australia.

When I started working at Abco, my first assignment was to create some promotional material for the upcoming ISSA exhibition.

Working with the marketing team, I was instrumental in improving the Enviroplus branding and design layout, beyond the previous catalogues that they had used.

The X-Range Concentrates and the solution dispensing system called RubiX gave me the inspiration to produce a new 'X'-shaped, angular layout, to highlight a stunning new look.
