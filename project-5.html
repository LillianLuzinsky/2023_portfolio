Indigenous Literacy Foundation

Abco, through Enviroplus, became a major supporter of the Indigenous Literacy Foundation (ILF) - an organisation involved in helping Aboriginal and Torres Strait Islanders get access to literature in their native language across Australia.

The project was to provide a brochure displaying some of Enviroplus products. A portion of the profits from sales of the advertised products goes towards supporting the ILF.

When producing Abco's visual branding within the ILF's brand guidelines, I was inspired to create a geometrical layout throughout to produce more visual impact.
